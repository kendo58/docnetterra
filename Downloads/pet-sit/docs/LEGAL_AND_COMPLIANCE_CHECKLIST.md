# Legal and Compliance Checklist

Track these launch requirements with legal counsel signoff.

## Policy Artifacts

- [ ] Terms of Service updated for paid booking/payment flows.
- [ ] Privacy Policy updated for payment, messaging, and admin moderation data handling.
- [ ] Refund/cancellation policy aligned with product behavior.
- [ ] Incident response policy reviewed and approved.

## Data and Security

- [ ] Data retention policy defined for bookings, messages, and audit logs.
- [ ] Access control policy for admin actions documented.
- [ ] Breach notification workflow and owner defined.
- [ ] Vendor/subprocessor inventory current (Supabase, Stripe, email provider, observability provider).

## Operational Readiness

- [ ] DPA/vendor agreements signed where required.
- [ ] Support process for privacy requests (access/delete/export) documented.
- [ ] Security contact and disclosure process published.
